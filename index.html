Destroy The Particles

Touch the screen to create an explosion. Particles that hit explosions will also explode creating chains.

Complete The Challenge

Beat the level minimum to continue. As the levels go on the explosions will get smaller increasing the difficulty.

Unlock Multiple Explosions

As the levels progress they will require more than just one explosion to complete the challenge.

Gravity Doesn't Suck

You can now use gravity to your advantage. Gravity fields attract particles and lock them into orbit until the gravity field becomes too weak and the particles are released.

Double The Trouble

You can now use two explosions at anytime to maximize the destruction.

Three Is More Fun

You can now use three explosions at anytime to maximize the destruction.

Four Is More

You can now use four explosions at anytime to maximize the destruction.

Pulse Particles

The pulse particle is pure white and packs a punch. It causes 1.5X destruction and double the points.
