Multisplode - Free Particle Explosion Game

About Multisplode

Multisplode is an engaging browser-based particle physics puzzle game that challenges players to think strategically. With 40 progressively challenging levels, players must use limited explosive devices to destroy all particles on the screen.

Game Features

  • 40 unique levels with increasing difficulty
  • Physics-based particle interactions and explosions
  • Multiple device types including shockwaves and gravity fields
  • Colorful particle effects and smooth animations
  • Progressive unlocking system
  • Optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers
  • No downloads or installations required
  • Free to play

How to Play

In Multisplode, your objective is to eliminate all particles from the playing field using a limited number of explosive devices. Each level presents a unique challenge with different particle configurations and device constraints.

Click or tap on the screen to place your devices strategically. Timing and positioning are crucial as chain reactions can help you complete levels more efficiently. Some particles pulse and expand, while devices create different types of explosions including shockwaves and gravity fields.

Game Mechanics

The game features realistic particle physics with collision detection, momentum, and gravity. Particles bounce off walls and each other, creating dynamic gameplay scenarios. Master the physics to create spectacular chain reactions!

Click the Play button to begin your explosive puzzle adventure. Progress through levels, unlock new challenges, and test your strategic thinking skills. The game runs entirely in your web browser with no downloads needed.
